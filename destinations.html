Adams Peak (Sri Pada)

A sacred mountain in Sri Lanka, famous for its breathtaking sunrise and spiritual significance. The climb is challenging but rewarding, offering unforgettable views at dawn.

  • Sunrise hike to the summit
  • Sacred footprint shrine
  • Stunning panoramic views

Little Adams Peak

A smaller, easier hike compared to Adam’s Peak, but equally beautiful. Perfect for catching views of Ella’s lush landscapes without the tough climb

  • Short, scenic hike
  • Breathtaking valley and mountain views
  • Close to Nine Arches Bridge & tea plantations

Horton Plains National Park

A highland plateau filled with unique wildlife, misty landscapes, and the famous Worlds End cliff. A must-visit for nature lovers and hikers.

  • Trek to Worlds End viewpoint
  • Visit Bakers Falls waterfall
  • Spot sambars, birds, and other wildlife
